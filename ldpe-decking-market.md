<p><p>The LDPE decking market is primarily divided into two types: capped composite and uncapped composite. Capped composite decking features a protective layer that enhances durability, resistance to stains, and fading, making it ideal for extreme weather. In contrast, uncapped composite decking lacks this protective coating, often resulting in lower costs but increased vulnerability to moisture and wear. Both options cater to different consumer preferences, emphasizing trade-offs between affordability and performance in outdoor decking solutions.</p></p>
