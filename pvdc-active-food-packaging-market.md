<p><strong>The PVDC Active Food Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVDC Latex</li><li>PVDC Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVDC active food packaging market primarily comprises two types: PVDC latex and PVDC resin. PVDC latex is a water-based dispersion used to create flexible and high-barrier coatings, enhancing the shelf life of packaged foods by preventing moisture and oxygen infiltration. On the other hand, PVDC resin is a solid polymer that provides excellent gas barrier properties, making it suitable for various packaging formats. Both types are essential in improving food preservation, reducing waste, and maintaining product quality.</p></p>
