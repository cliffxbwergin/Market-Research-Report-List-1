<p><p>Heavy duty loading ramps are essential for transporting goods, and they come primarily in two types: hydraulic and pneumatic. Hydraulic ramps utilize fluid pressure to lift and lower the platform, offering precise control and high weight capacity, making them ideal for heavy loads. Pneumatic ramps operate using compressed air, providing a lighter and often more portable option. Both types enhance efficiency in loading and unloading applications, catering to diverse industrial needs while ensuring safety and durability.</p></p>
