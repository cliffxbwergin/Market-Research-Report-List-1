<p><p>Flat-film extrusion lines are categorized into vertical and horizontal market types based on their configuration and application in manufacturing. Vertical flat-film extrusion lines utilize a vertical arrangement, allowing for efficient gravity feeding of raw materials, often resulting in space savings and enhanced material flow. In contrast, horizontal lines feature a lateral layout, suitable for larger output capacities and easier accessibility for maintenance. Both types cater to diverse industries like packaging and agriculture, depending on production requirements and available floor space.</p></p>
