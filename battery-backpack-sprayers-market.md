<p><p>Battery backpack sprayers are divided into two main types based on the battery technology used: lithium-ion battery sprayers and lead-acid battery sprayers. Lithium-ion sprayers are known for their lightweight, longer run times, and faster charging capabilities, making them ideal for extended use and mobility. In contrast, lead-acid battery sprayers are typically heavier, offer a lower cost, but may have shorter run times and longer charging periods. Both types cater to various agricultural and landscaping needs, influencing user choice based on performance and budget.</p></p>
